Fennec Fox

Vulpes zerda

What are they?

The fennec fox or fennec (Vulpes zerda) is a small nocturnal fox found in the Sahara of North Africa.

Diet

They forage for plants but also eat rodents, eggs, reptiles, and insects.

Lifestyle

A fennec fox's typical den is dug in sand, either in open areas or places sheltered by plants with stable sand dunes considered to be their ideal habitat.
