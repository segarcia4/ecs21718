Famous Social Activists

Martin Luther King Jr

M.L.K. Leader in the Civil Rights Movement


Martin Luther King was an American Baptist minister and activist who became the most visible spokesperson and leader in the civil rights movement.
He had a great deal of influence on American society in the 1950s and 1960s.
His strong belief in non-violent protest helped set the tone of the movement.
